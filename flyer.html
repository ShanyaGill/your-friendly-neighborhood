Your Friendly Neighborhood

What We Do

Your Friendly Neighborhood is a hands-on group where students identify real issues in San José and work together to solve them. We focus on challenges in our community—such as sanitation, bike safety, and policy concerns—and reach out to the Youth Advisory Council, city officials, council members, and local organizations to take action.

Through this process, students gain the tools to connect with decision-makers, present ideas, and make tangible changes in their community.

Why Join?

What I like most about this club is that the ideas brought forward by its members are always valued and taken into consideration. Your ideas will be presented as yours, and with the support of the club, they can turn into real changes. Here, you truly have the chance to make a difference.
