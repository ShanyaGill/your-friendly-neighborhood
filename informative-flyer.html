Our Mission

Your Friendly Neighborhood is a hands-on civic engagement club where students identify real issues in San Jose and learn to solve them through action. We don't just talk about problems—we create real solutions through policy advocacy, community partnerships, and strategic improvements.

What We Do

Analyze & Advocate

Identify community issues and develop policy solutions by connecting with city officials and youth commissioners.

Connect & Engage

Build relationships with local leaders and organizations to turn ideas into real policy changes.

Install & Improve

Make physical improvements like safety equipment and cleaning supplies—all branded with YFN stickers.

Learn & Lead

Develop real-world skills in research, communication, project management, and civic leadership.

Who We Partner With

City Councilmembers

Work directly with San Jose City Council members across all 10 districts to advocate for policy changes and community improvements.

Youth Advisory Councils

Collaborate with District Youth Advisory Councils where teens provide input on policies affecting young people.

Local Organizations

Partner with community groups and non-profits to amplify our impact and learn from experienced advocates.

City Commissioners

Connect with city commissioners and staff who support student-led initiatives and civic engagement projects.

Ready to Make Real Change?

Join students who don't just complain—we advocate for solutions! Learn civic engagement, connect with city officials, and build a portfolio of real community impact.
