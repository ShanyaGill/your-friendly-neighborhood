Our Mission

Your Friendly Neighborhood is a hands-on civic engagement group where students identify real issues in San Jose and learn how to solve them through action. Members analyze challenges, brainstorm solutions, and practice advocacy by reaching out to youth commissioners, city officials, and local organizations. We don't just focus on learning about government—this club focuses on doing, equipping students with tools to connect with decision-makers, present ideas, and make tangible change in their community.
