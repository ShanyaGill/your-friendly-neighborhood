Our Mission

Your Friendly Neighborhood is a hands-on civic engagement group where students identify real issues in San Jose and learn how to solve them through action. Members analyze challenges, brainstorm solutions, and practice advocacy by reaching out to youth commissioners, city officials, and local organizations.
